# A Cybersecurity Lab Explained Like You're 10! 🔒👾

Hey there! Let's talk about a cool cybersecurity lab called the "Blue-Team Dashboard Analysis with Wazuh." It's like playing detective in a digital world.

Imagine you have a super-smart security camera system for your house (that's Wazuh). The goal of this lab was to set up this system and use it to find out if anyone tried to break into our house (brute force attack)! 🏠🔒

First, we made some pretend bad guys try to break in by guessing passwords over and over again (simulated brute force attack). Then, we looked at the camera footage (Wazuh dashboard views) to see if anyone was trying to get in. 🕵️‍♂️📺

We found that there were 9 times when someone tried to break in but couldn't! That means they guessed wrong too many times and got locked out. But, no one actually managed to break in (no successful logins). 🚫🔒

This is important because it helps us learn how to protect our house better by finding ways to spot when someone is trying to break in and stop them before they can get inside! 🛡️💪

Now, the lab is complete and ready to be shared with others so they can learn too! 📚🎉
