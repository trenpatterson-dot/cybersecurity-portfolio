# Cybersecurity Lab: Soc Alert Triage Failed Login

A cybersecurity lab is like a playground for super-smart detectives who fight against computer bad guys! In this lab, called the `Soc Alert Triage Failed Login`, we used special tools (Wireshark and Wazuh) to solve a mystery.

The mystery was about failed login attempts. Just like when you try too many times to guess your friend's secret password, these attempts could be someone trying to break into a computer system. Our job was to find out what was happening and why it mattered.

We looked at the clues (evidence) from our tools and figured out how the alert was reviewed, what evidence was collected, what findings were confirmed, and how the activity was classified. This information is all written down in a special document called `HANDOFF.md`.

Now that we've solved this mystery, it's important to keep improving our detective skills so we can protect computers from bad guys even better!
